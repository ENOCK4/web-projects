Historical background of St Aloysious Voc S S

St Aloysious is a day and boarding mixed secondary school, with both "O" and "A" level. The school is a privately owned institution fully registered with the Minitry of Education, Science, Technology and Sports. The school started on 3th Feb, 1994.

Location

Our school is in Kooki-county, Rakai District. It is approximately 1/2km away from lwamaggwa town and 20km from kyotera town see map

School Vision

To become a centre of excellence in all round education values.

School Mission

To disseminate knowledge to students on an all round education basis.

School Philosophy

"Obstacles in the struggle succumb to resilience"


School Virtues

The school culture hinges on five pillars which are;

  • Discipline
  • Hard work
  • Resilience
  • Integrity
  • Excellence

School Motto

"Struggle for Development "

The motto emphasizes hard work, resilience, discpline and never giving up on excellence.
