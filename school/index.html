St Aoysious Voc S S Background

view details

St Aloysious voc S.S is a mixed, boarding secondary school for O’ and A’ Level, with excellent boarding facilities.St Aloysious Voc S.S is fully registered by the Ministry of Education and sports since 4th February 1991 under a UNEB centre number U0665.The school is interdenominational. Students attend prayers according to their religious affiliation declared by their parents on admission. E.g. Protestants have a chaplain, Catholics have a chaplain, Muslims attend Juma prayers every Friday and Adventists are also catered for as well.A high quality education is one of the most important things that we can provide a child. Exposure to a variety of topics, people and adventures all contribute to a fulfilling school experience like St Aloysious Voc S.S

The School Laboratory

Our laboratory is well stocked with standard equipments used during science practical.

The laboratory operates from Monday to Saturday.

Students must exercise maximum care of the apparatus to avoid breakages. In case it happens the person involved compensates it.

Sports

The school support students who have various talents that footballer, netball, volleyball, athletics and many others .And alsothe school offers bursaries who are talented

For the previous years the school has been participarting and performing well in copa coco cola competitions

The School Library

The school has a well stocked library.

The library opens at 8:00am and closes at 5:00pm, Monday to Friday. You only borrow a particular text book after presenting an identity card and any lost books are replaced by parents after giving the student a punishment.
